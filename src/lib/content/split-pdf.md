## Split PDF Files – Extract Pages Directly in Your Browser

The **Split PDF** tool allows you to **split, extract, and reorganize PDF pages directly in your browser**. All processing is done **client-side**, ensuring your PDF files are never uploaded to any server and never leave your device.

This tool is built for users who need a **secure PDF splitter online**, especially when working with confidential or sensitive documents.

If you are looking for **split PDF online without upload** or **client-side PDF page extractor**, this tool fits that use case.


## Why Use a Client-Side PDF Splitter?

### 1. Privacy-First PDF Page Extraction
All PDF splitting and extraction happens inside your browser using your device’s memory.

- Files stay on your device
- No internet transfer of document data
- Safe for tax records, medical files, legal contracts, and personal documents

Your data is never exposed to external servers.


### 2. Fast and Instant Processing
There is no upload queue or server-side processing delay.

- Pages are extracted using your device’s CPU
- Results are generated almost instantly
- Extracted PDFs are bundled into a ZIP file for easy download

This makes the tool significantly faster than traditional online PDF splitters.


### 3. Flexible Page Range Selection
The tool supports advanced page range syntax, allowing you to extract exactly what you need.

Supported formats include:
- Single pages (e.g. `1`)
- Page ranges (e.g. `1-5`)
- Multiple selections (e.g. `1, 3, 7-10`)

Each selected page or range is exported as a separate PDF file.


## How to Split PDF Files Safely

1. **Select PDF File**  
   Drag and drop a PDF file into the tool or click to browse your device.

2. **Enter Page Numbers or Ranges**  
   Specify the pages you want to extract using commas and ranges.

3. **Split and Download**  
   Click **Split & Export ZIP**. The extracted PDFs will be generated and downloaded as a ZIP archive.

Original PDF files remain unchanged.
